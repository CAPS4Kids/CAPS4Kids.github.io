Founder of CAPS4Kids

Hello Everyone! I'm Kushal Chigati, the founder of Chess and Programming For Kids. I'm currently a freshman at the University of California - Santa Cruz, and I am studying Computer Science. I created CAPS4Kids to bring the chess and programming communities of my home town together to create something absolutely remarkable! As for my credentials, I have a United States Chess Federation rating of 1180 and am extremely proficient in Python, Java, HTML, CSS, Swift, and C++. Students who participate in this program are guaranteed to benefit, whether it may be to learn new skills in programming and chess or to hone their existing programming and chess skills!
